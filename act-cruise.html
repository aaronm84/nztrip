What it is

Board late afternoon as the day boats leave, cruise the full fjord to the Tasman mouth, kayak off the stern if the water’s calm, dinner aboard, and a night anchored beneath 4,000-foot walls. Dawn on deck with the fjord entirely to ourselves is the image the whole trip is built around.

For the two of us

Private ensuite cabins on both boats. GF and all three allergies go on the booking — the galley handles it well with notice, and the seafood course means shrimp WILL be present; the crew needs to know. Top-deck hot tub on the Jewel: swimwear is in the packing list for a reason.

What people say

"The best thing we did in New Zealand" is the modal review for both operators. The overnight consistently outrates day cruises — it’s the empty-fjord hours that do it. Rain reviews are, if anything, better: the waterfalls multiply by the hundred.
