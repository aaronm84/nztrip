The walk

Three swing bridges up a glacial valley to a lake where icebergs calve off the Hooker Glacier with Aoraki filling the sky dead ahead. It is, famously, the best easy walk in the country — a flat track through scenery that has no business being this accessible.

What walkers say

Thousands of reviews, one theme: the payoff-to-effort ratio is absurd. Late-autumn walkers add that the crowds vanish and the light goes gold. Recent note: the track has had partial closures for bridge work — check DOC status the week before.

Our notes

Late-day light on Aoraki is the photograph. Walk it the afternoon we arrive, spa bath after, stars after that — the perfect Mt Cook sequence. It is fully exposed: wind jackets even on blue days.
