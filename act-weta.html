Weta Workshop Tour

The workshop that built Middle-earth’s armour, creatures and props — tours run by working artists among actual film artefacts.

Duration~1.5–2 hours
Cost~NZ$60–100/person — verify
WhereMiramar, 20 min from town
BookA few weeks ahead is fine

What it is

The workshop that built Middle-earth’s armour, creatures and props — tours run by working artists among actual film artefacts. The deep-lore companion piece to standing in the real filming locations.

For the two of us

Indoor, unhurried, and a perfect foul-weather anchor for the Wellington day. Pair with Te Papa if the famous wind is up.

What people say

Consistently one of Wellington’s top-rated things to do; the guides — many of them film crew — are the difference. Book a morning slot and keep the afternoon loose.
