What it is

The "Valley of the Vines" — Central Otago Pinot Noir country at its autumn-gold peak. Gibbston Valley Winery’s cave tour ends with a tasting deep in the schist; the restaurant does long lunches by the fire; neighbouring cellar doors (Peregrine, Chard Farm) make it a crawl if the afternoon stretches.

For the two of us

Wine is naturally GF — this is the trip’s zero-stress food day. The cheese course is the one dairy checkpoint: their sheep and goat cheeses keep her in bounds. Landing-day pacing: one cave tour, one long lunch, no heroics.

What people say

The cave tasting is the consistently-praised move over a standard cellar door — atmosphere does half the work. Autumn reviewers rave about the poplar colour on the drive alone.
