The walk

From The Divide the track zigzags up through silver beech to an alpine plateau of tarns and boardwalks with a genuine 360°: the Darran Mountains, the Hollyford Valley running to the sea, and three watersheds parting at your feet. The last stretch of the Routeburn we’ll touch.

What walkers say

Routinely rated the best half-day on the Milford Road — "maximum alpine per step." Reviewers’ one warning is fog: the plateau can white out, so take the first clear window rather than saving it for the return drive.

Our notes

Do it on the way IN to Milford (day 9) while the forecast is known. The tarns mirror the Darrans on still mornings — camera weather insurance is the dry bag.
