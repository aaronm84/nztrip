What it is

Deliberately unplanned. The menu, in ascending adrenaline: Onsen Hot Pools (private cedar tubs over the Shotover — book this one ahead), the Skyline Gondola and luge, a Shotover Jet canyon run, or the original Kawarau Bridge bungy. Or: nothing, slowly, by the lake.

For the two of us

This is also where any weather-cancelled day gets its second chance — Key Summit fogged out, Gibbston rained off, whatever needs a rerun. Shoulder-season availability means almost nothing needs pre-booking except the Onsen tubs.

What people say

The Onsen pools are the sleeper hit for couples in reviews — cedar tubs, mountain view, nobody else. The jet boat is the classic for a reason; the luge is pure dumb fun.
